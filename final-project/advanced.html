Advanced Program

Welcome to the advanced program! Below are your exercises and instructions to progress further.

Exercise 1: Barbell Squats

Leverage the bar onto your rear delts, walk out with the barbell from the rack, take a big breath and hold air into stomach to brace core, keeping back flat and chest up, lower yourself as if sitting in a chair and stand back up. Let air out at the top. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 2: Leg Press

Sit in leg press, place feet on leg press platform at shoulder width. Press weight up, remove racks, then lower weight controlled bringing knees as close to your chest without your butt leaving the seat, then press back up. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 3: Leg Extensions

Sit all the way back in leg extension machine fitting legs behind the pads at your ankles. Extend legs out until knees lock and quadriceps flex, control weight back down. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 4: Ab Crunch Machine

Sit in ab machine, grab straps and pull them over your shoulders. Hold the straps firmly as you curl your body forward as far as you can and then control the weight to the starting position. Repeat until failure.
