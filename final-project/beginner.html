Beginner Program

Welcome to the beginner program! Below are your exercises and instructions to get started.

Exercise 1: Skullcrushers

Lay flat on your back on a bench. Use an ez bar with a narrow grip. Keep elbows pointed to ceiling. Start with bar above forehead and press vertically with elbows in a fixed position. Control the eccentric letting tricep stretch until bar reaches forehead.

Exercise 2: Cable Curls

Use an ez bar with a medium width grip. Keep elbows to the side of your torso and use strict movements, just bending at the elbow to curl the bar up towards your chest. Control the eccentric letting the bicep stretch to the bottom.

Exercise 3: Dumbbell Crossbody Curls

Start by holding a dumbbell in each hand at your side, bending at the elbow, curl the dumbbell in the middle of your chest and lower the weight back to your side with control.

Exercise 4: "Drive the Bus" Superset with Front Raises

Start by holding a plate out in front of you parallel to your shoulders and the ground. Turn the plate side to side as if you're turning a steering wheel back and forth until fatigued. Superset this movement with front raises by raising the weight from the rested bottom position back to the parallel to shoulder position at the top until failure.

Exercise 5: Inverted Rows

Position bar at an incline that you can comfortably keep body flat and core engaged while holding the bar outside shoulder width, lower yourself until arms are fully extended and then pull your chest toward the bar. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 1: Hammer Strength Incline Chest Press

Position seat so that the top of your chest is even with the handles. Space hands evenly on each handle. Keeping your shoulder blades on the back rest, press the weight out in front of you then control back down to your chest. Repeat until failure.
