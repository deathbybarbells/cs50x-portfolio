Elite Program

Welcome to the elite program! Below are your exercises and instructions to progress further.

Exercise 1: Barbell Bench Press

Lay down on benchpress. Position your eyes vertical underneath the bar on the rack. Place hands on the bar spaced evenly apart outside shoulder width. Wedge your shoulder blades into the bench, digging your lats down towards your glutes, creating an arch. Lift bar off of rack then proceed to lower the barbell in a slight arch to the bottom of your chest and then press back up in a slight arching motion to starting position. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 2: Heavy Barbell Bench Press

Lay down on benchpress. Position your eyes vertical underneath the bar on the rack. Place hands on the bar spaced evenly apart outside shoulder width. Wedge your shoulder blades into the bench, digging your lats down towards your glutes, creating an arch. Lift bar off of rack then proceed to lower the barbell in a slight arch to the bottom of your chest and then press back up in a slight arching motion to starting position. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 3: Deficit Deadlifts

Stand on a raised platform or plate. Grab barbell outside shoulder width. Starting in a hinged position, keeping your back flat, chest out, and core engaged by breathing air into stomach, pull the bar off of the floor with leg drive and then hinge your hips forward and stand up until straight, lower bar controlled with a proper form hinge to the floor. Repeat until failure.

Exercise 4: Deadlift PR

Grab barbell outside shoulder width with a switch grip. Starting in a hinged position, keeping your back flat, chest out, and core engaged by breathing air into stomach, pull the bar off of the floor with leg drive and then hinge your hips forward and stand up until straight, lower bar controlled with a proper form hinge to the floor. Turn supersaiyan to lift this and reach a personal record.
